KS Hotel

Prudential

One of the most important representatives of Polish interwar architecture. He became an important symbol of the revival of Szczecin after the war. Towering majestically over the capital, visible from a distance of up to 20 km. Its modern steel and reinforced concrete structure was a response to the strong and dynamic urbanization of the Szczecin agglomeration and a proof of the mastery of pre-war engineering. Today it is one of the most modern hotels in Poland.
